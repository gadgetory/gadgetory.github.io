insanely bright has really good viewing angles and everything is sharp though as you've seen with some tablets colors can be a tiny bit over contrasted and saturated the one speaker on the device is really nothing special it's just a single mono speaker and it sounds good but it never really gets very loud starting this week the transformer infinity comes with Android 4.1 jelly bean it's now the pretty great overall the transformer pad infinity is kind of a hard product to review it's great for watching movies or anything else that takes advantage of the 1920 x 1200 screen and Google now is
