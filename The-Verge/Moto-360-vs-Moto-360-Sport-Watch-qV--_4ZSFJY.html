Moto 360 vs. Moto 360 Sport Watch

this is the new moto 360 sport watch it's a sporty er version of the regular Moto 360 smartwatch - which came out earlier this year this sport watch costs two hundred and ninety nine dollars and it's although like the regular Moto 360 there are more than a dozen other digital watch faces you can use if you prefer those also the display of the sport watch has a different hybrid display technology Moto 360 the Sport version has optical heart rate sensors that read heart rate through your wrist but the Moto 360 sport watch has built-in GPS which the regular Moto 360 doesn't have and a sport with mixed use but it's safe to say that if your workouts are super long and you're using GPS during that time on the sport watch the battery life on that one will drain more quickly so the idea here is that the regular Moto 360 is something you'd wear throughout the day but it's not really meant for workouts whereas the Moto 360 sport is meant to be the kind of watch you'd wear for workouts and
