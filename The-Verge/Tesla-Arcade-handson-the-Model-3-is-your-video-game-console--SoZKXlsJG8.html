- It's Nick Statt with The Verge, and we're checking out Tesla Arcade on a Tesla Model 3. It's the company's new video game hub built right into its touch screen. So Tesla has actually had video games built into their center screen here since last August. They used to hide it in a secret Easter egg menu so you would tap this Tesla bar up here, and you would pull down and it hid a whole bunch of stuff here, so not just video games but just like funny fart mode thing here, you can turn your car into a Mars Rover here. Effectively, Tesla is telling you, don't hit the accelerator when you're playing a game. You can hit the brake pedal and it will work in this racing game. But you're not supposed to hit the accelerator and also you have to be prepared for the full control of the car to resume when you exit the game. It's gonna be disabled while you're playing the game.
