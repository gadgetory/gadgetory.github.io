usable friendly phone designed for everybody at the same time though there's no question that the Moto X feels like a premium device it has a different feel from the solid metallic HTC One but even it and there are seven options for the buttons on the side and the accent ring around the camera as well being able to customize the phone probably won't make or break the success of the Moto X but it might make buyers consider it when they would have just passed over a black version on the shelves there are some great combinations and some hideous ones and it's gonna be really interesting to see how Motorola and carriers show those options to customers but I love the idea of being able to treat my phone like my car or my cool Nike hyssops you customize the whole thing through a really simple
