slightly lighter it weighs about 7 ounces instead of last year 7.5 and it has a slightly lighter grey stripe around the edges but otherwise they're literally exactly the same Hardware they even have the same screen the 800 x 600 pixel eating display and that's basically the same as every other you book reader out there but the only problem with this devices screen is that there's a screen totally uniform you get a really bright stripe at the top and then it gets dark and then brighter again but for the most part it works well and it definitely makes the device readable in the dark you turn it on by pressing and holding the end button at the bottom for two or three seconds or there's a menu item for doing it it's really easy and you can toggle the levels of brightness I wound up
