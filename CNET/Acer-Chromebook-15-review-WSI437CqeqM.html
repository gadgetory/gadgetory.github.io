Acer Chromebook 15 review

2017-12-08
the Acer Chromebook 15 is a big laptop with perfect pricing starting at 399 dollars it's a decent performer that packs a 15.6 inch touchscreen loudspeakers Blog battery life and a relatively slim and lightweight design especially for a large budget laptop it has a stylish aluminum lid and metal chassis with thick silver bezels that resemble a MacBook the backlit keyboard is comfortable to type on and the touchpad is silky smooth with chamfered edges to match the rest of the laptops design it definitely looks more expensive than it is running Chrome OS the Acer Chromebook was built for using Google's cloud-based apps like Gmail Google Docs and Chrome browser it also has access to the Google Play Store that means you can download apps like on an Android device so instead of just using the laptop for browsing the web checking email and streaming video you can post to Instagram download video for offline watching using the Netflix app or download games to play when there isn't a Wi-Fi network available the 15.6 inch screen has a full 1080p HD resolution that's nice and sharp although a little dull in color saturation viewing angles are solid in the laptops 180 degree hinge helped achieve an optimal viewing angle there are two speakers located on the left and right edges of the keyboard they're surprisingly loud and crisp and easily fill a room however like most laptop speakers they lack bass since the laptops big screen and loud speakers make it great for watching video it's unfortunate that the laptop doesn't have a 360 degree hinge that would allow it to prop up like a tent or flip the keyboard over as a stand for a better viewing angle the Acer Chromebook 15s performance was as expected based on its internal specs it smoothly handled many open chrome tabs and a few apps open in the background before it started to slow down battery life was impressive lasting an entire workday on a full charge in battery testing looping a 720p video it lasted 13 hours and 45 minutes if you're interested in buying a Chromebook with a big screen you don't have a large variety to choose from thankfully the Acer Chromebook 15 is an excellent value for the casual user making it a very easy choice to make
