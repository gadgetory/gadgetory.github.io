Prevent YouTube music from killing your data plan (Tech Minute)

2016-04-08
if you use the YouTube Music app to access YouTube's huge library of songs you might be running up your data usage and with enough listening you might end up going over your data cap to prevent this from happening youtube music now has an option that lets you stream over Wi-Fi only this means you won't be able to stream songs over the network but it could save you money in the end to enable the feature tap the hamburger button in the upper left corner of the app and tap settings then tap the toggle switch to stream over Wi-Fi only youtube music isn't the only app that could kill your data plan Facebook Instagram snapchat and the full YouTube app all have options to only load media when you're connected to Wi-Fi I'd especially recommend enabling this for facebook since auto playing videos in the feed will use up a lot of data for more on how to enable these settings visit cnet com in San Francisco I'm Sharon Profis cnet.com for CBS News
