How to afford the new iPhone X (CNET How To)

2017-09-14
Apple says its new iPhone 10 is its most advanced iPhone to date but it's also its most expensive so if you just have to have it but you're not quite sure how to afford it here are three things you can do most phone carriers offer payment plans that allow you to pay off your device over a period of time you can then swap out for the latest and greatest after twelve months or as long as 50% of the device has been paid off programs like t-mobile's jump on demand give you three free upgrades a year or if you'd rather not be tied down to a specific carrier you can always try Apple's own upgrade program apple's program works a lot like the wireless carriers the difference is you get an unlocked phone to use with any carrier in the US and you get a year's worth of application surance that would then for you up to shop for a better deal on a carrier and save even more money if you're looking for a single line of service Sprint's free year of unlimited is tough to beat you'll still have to pay fees and taxes and not all phones work on it but it's free virgin also has a similar deal but for a dollar a year same goes for family plans Sprint and cricket are the current cheapest and before you toss out that old phone make sure it's helping you pay for the new one just because it's old to you doesn't mean someone else won't be ready to snatch it right up Craigslist or Ebay may give you the best price because you're dealing directly with the buyer but you have to arrange delivery and payment if you want someone else to do the dirty work for you Best Buy Amazon and Apple are just a few places that will buy them from you but you'll get gift cards instead of cash echo ATMs give you cash but may not give you top dollar and if all else fails you can always give up that Starbucks addiction for nine months or forego the gym for a year
