Vizio announces new audio systems

2015-04-14
television manufacturer Vizio is announced 7 new sound bar and sound based systems which offer a much sleeker look than before the sound bars now have a black and brushed aluminum finish which extends to the wireless subwoofer and surrounds if included the main model is a 38-inch sound bar which comes without a sub with the sub and with surround speakers the sound bar age culminates in a top-of-the-line SB 4051 which is a 5.1 model which adds hdmi connectivity and a slightly larger subwoofer meanwhile the sound basis come in two different models with and without an onboard subwoofer both models have been shortened in height and are designed to support a 32 inch TV on top or to slip underneath the E&amp;M series TV's most of the sound bars and sound basis are shipping from April 2015
