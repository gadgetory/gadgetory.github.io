Mid range blender gets the job done eventually

2016-08-17
it might sound like a plane taking off in your kitchen but rest assured that's just this Sultan Harley Pasternak blender it was developed by a fitness guru to compete with high-end models like Vitamix and Blendtec at a much lower price and the specs stack up well it has 1500 watts of power and a 67 ounce jar both of those are roughly the same as Vitamix and Blendtec models we tested but those models cost four or five hundred dollars this is two hundred and fifty so that's a really appealing price but here's the bad news this blender is much slower than the high-powered competition you'll also have to help it out with tougher tasks by stirring shifting the speeds around to get the tougher ingredients down towards the blades and its really a pain to get thick ingredients out from the bottom of the jar and like I said really loud if you have the patience to wait it can do everything Vitamix and Blendtec can it made a nice creamy pesto it even turned whole almonds into almond butter and pulverize the whole block of cheese it does ship with this tamper if you need help keeping ingredients moving and rounding out a nice feature list it also comes with this to-go cup so given the price I'd lean towards this model over Vitamix or Blendtec but I wouldn't pick it over the ninja Ultima which also makes high-powered blending affordable and outperforms this guy still the sultan Harley Pasternak blender is confident enough to be worth your consideration just spend a few extra bucks on ear plugs to go along with them
