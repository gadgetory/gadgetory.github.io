Tech Minute - Websites stuffed with holiday recipes

2014-12-17
a great website for recipe inspiration is smitten kitchen the site isn't new but has gained fame for the homespun recipes and musings of supremely talented home cook deb perelman new recipes and photos are posted every couple of days but you can also search for a recipe by season or type of ingredient food52 has less of a folksy personal feel but offers a wide variety of recipes click on a thumbnail of an appealing photo or choose from the topics and columns list for more ideas and inspiration one great feature is the food52 hotline where you can ask or answer a cooking related question finally Whole Foods is known for their supermarkets and quality goods but their website also features delicious recipes search from nearly 4000 recipes and once you find one you like you can add the ingredients to your whole food shopping list that you can build through the site itself in San Francisco I'm Kara Tsuboi cnet.com for CBS News
