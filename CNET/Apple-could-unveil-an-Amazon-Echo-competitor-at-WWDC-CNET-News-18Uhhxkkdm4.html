Apple could unveil an Amazon Echo competitor at WWDC (CNET News)

2017-05-01
Ceri may soon be giving Alexa a run for her money as rumors heat up about an apple echo competitor coming this summer a report by kti security analyst Ming sequel published in MacRumors states there's more than a 50% chance that Apple will announce its first home AI product at its Developers Conference in June and start selling by the second half of the year Apple Siri speaker would be marketed as a higher-end version of the echo with a subwoofer and better sound system which also means higher price tag more than the $179 you'd pay for an echo long time Apple leaker Sonny Dixon also tweeted about the series speaker last week saying it would run a variant of iOS and carry some sort of beats technology and earlier rumors had pointed at a potential camera on this device that would be able to identify individual users to adjust the room to their preferences and adjust lighting and music but even with all these extra bells and whistles there's no hiding that Apple is arriving late to the home assistant game and would be playing catch-up to Alexa and Google assistant who have already carved their way onto people's countertops
