LeEco Le S3 phone review

2016-12-16
the LA Ecola s3 is a $250 phone that packs Android 6.0 marshmallow into a 5.5 inch package the device is powered by a Snapdragon 650 2 processor and a 3 ampere hour battery you also get a 16 megapixel camera that can record 4k video and an 8 megapixel front facing shooter to snap selfies it has dual SIM capabilities and 32 gigs of built-in memory but there is no expandable storage the phone also doesn't have NFC or a headphone jack so you'll either have to use a wireless Bluetooth headset or a dongle to listen to your music and calls the s3 has a clunky interface there's no app drawer and your recent apps are merged together with quick settings in one messy page the echos live broadcasting video service which sits in your home screen - and can't be removed also needs work though it has clips that you can browse from time to time there's nothing here that I'd actually really want to watch la echos other video app and the love view video curator don't have compelling content either at $250 the phone is definitely affordable and maybe we're considering if you have a tight budget but know that there are better phones out there that cost the same like the Motorola g4 plus and the Asus zenfone 3
