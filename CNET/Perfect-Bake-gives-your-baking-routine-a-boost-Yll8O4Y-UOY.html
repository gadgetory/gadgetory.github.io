Perfect Bake gives your baking routine a boost

measuring is less precise and you don't really want to be messing around with that when it comes to baking it's really more of a science than an art another way they want to simplify the baking headphone jack of your phone or tablet and start baking and it makes the process so simple I think this would be particularly good for families with young children for a really interactive fun baking
