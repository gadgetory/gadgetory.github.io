Electrolux EFLW317TIW washing machine review

2017-12-18
this 879 dollar front load washing machine is one of electrolux's most minimal models it only has 5 cleaning cycles and 3 settings each for temperatures soil level and spin speed it's white finish looks fine but I would like to see more contrast between its light gray display panel and the rest of the washer even so Electrolux at least kept a couple of highlights from its higher end line up the circular digital display and the delay start option that lets you schedule your wash cycle to start anywhere from 30 minutes to 12 hours later it also opted for an integrated touch screen over buttons and dials which gives it a slightly more modern look inside the detergent drawer you'll also find compartments for adding liquid bleach and fabric softener and the drum inside has a 4.3 cubic foot capacity the biggest selling point though is its cleaning performance this washer did a great job removing stains just keep in mind that you can get Electrolux is even better performing 400 series washer for just 20 bucks more having said that this is a great washer if you want something simple that gets the job done
