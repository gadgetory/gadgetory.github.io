CNET How To - An easy way to stream Spotify and Amazon Video to your Apple TV

2015-11-02
the new Apple TV doesn't include either Spotify or Amazon video but there is still a way you can get them on your TV first you need to make sure airplay is enabled the Apple TV must also be connected to the same network as your Mac or iOS device on your Mac open the web browser and head to Amazon's video page then select the airplay logo in the top corner of your Macs menu bar it should be next to the Wi-Fi icon after choosing your Apple TV device your computer will then be mirrored to your TV now just go ahead and play the Amazon video and just be sure to make it fullscreen this can also be done on your iOS device simply open the Amazon video app play the show or movie you would like to watch then tap the airplay logo and select your Apple TV the method is pretty much the same for Spotify either mirror your computer screen to the Apple TV and open Spotify or from your iOS device open the Spotify app select the song you want to play click where it says devices available choose more devices and select your Apple TV both of these methods can also be used on older Apple TV models for more tips and tricks like this one be sure to check out how to see Netcom you can also reach out to me on Twitter with any questions or comments I'm Dan grasshopper CNET thanks for watching Oh
