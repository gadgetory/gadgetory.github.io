ZTE Blade V8 Pro review

2017-03-04
the ZTE Blade v8 pro is an affordable mid-range phone that you can get unlocked for only 230 dollars it's equipped with a 5.5 inch display and has two 13 megapixel cameras on the back with this dual camera setup you can take different kinds of artsy photos one setting gives your images a bokeh effect similar to the one on the Apple iPhone 7 plus it doesn't work as smooth but it lets you blur the background or foreground after you take an image for a dramatic look because one of the shooters is a monochrome camera you can take some fancy black-and-white shots - or pictures with just one pop of color there's also a live photo option which again we've seen on iPhones or you can take short jiffy photos that have movement one of the best things about the phone is its battery life it has a 3.14 ampere-hour non-removable battery and during our lab tests it clocked in 14 hours of continuous video playback on airplane mode that's an excellent time and it has quick charging to the phone has a fingerprint reader for user security NFC and dual-sim capabilities as well unfortunately it runs Android marshmallow which isn't the most recent version of the OS at six and a half ounces is also pretty heavy in the hand and pocket and its back cover collects a lot of fingerprints and smudges that are difficult to wipe off but again the phone is only $230 which is a good deal if you want a cheap Android device plus with its impressive battery life you'll be able to get through a workday without having to recharge it all the time
