Zvox SoundBase 350 is decent for a budget TV speaker

2015-05-22
buying a new speaker for your TV can be tough as there are so many options out there but when it comes to building speakers that you can put your TV on top of there's one brand that was their first Z box the Z Box sound bass 350 is the company's newest budget model and for only a little over $200 it gives you a few useful features and decent sound quality this is a pretty small bass at only 2 feet across but it's well built and should be able to hold TVs up to 50 inches in screen size it may not be much to look at but it has some touches that competitors do miss such as an alphanumeric display and controls on the unit itself this speaker is designed to be hooked up via an optical cable to your TV and can decode Dolby soundtracks it also has Bluetooth connectivity so you can stream music from your phone but if you want more features than that you should definitely pay a lot more for the price so this is a decent performer with the voice enhancing functions particularly helpful for the hearing-impaired and it's able to put out a good amount of bass for such a small size sure it's not that great at music so if you want that investigate the sps bo3 from Pioneer instead while you still can but if you just want to hear your TV better there's not much else to compete with this at the price this is ty Pendlebury for cnet.com
