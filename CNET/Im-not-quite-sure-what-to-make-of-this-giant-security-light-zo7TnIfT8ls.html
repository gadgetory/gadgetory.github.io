I'm not quite sure what to make of this giant security light

2017-03-09
the zmodo torch Pro is a two-hundred-dollar smart home kit that includes a wired motion detector equipped doorbell a 720p high-definition security camera built into a color-changing outdoor rated LED and a plug-in Wi-Fi range extender but there's a huge problem with this device it's absolutely huge it didn't fit in my porch lights at home it doesn't fit in the porch lights at the scene at smart home as you can see and even after a trip to the home-improvement store only the largest models they carried would fit the torch pro is a cool concept though because the doorbell and the camera light hybrid device worked together to watch over your front door the doorbell detects motion sends you a push alert and pulls up a live feed of the activity happening outside from there you can use the two-way audio function to talk to the person you can also schedule the light to turn on at certain times keep in mind that the camera automatically switches to night vision mode when the LED is on and back to regular day mode when it's off all of this worked fine but the initial configuration was seriously confusing to the extent that I called customer service to help me pair the devices but even after setup the cluttered app design made it hard to find what I was looking for the torch pro also doesn't work with any third-party devices given that the app is hard to navigate and the camera LED won't fit in most porch lights I definitely can't recommend this emoto torch pro
