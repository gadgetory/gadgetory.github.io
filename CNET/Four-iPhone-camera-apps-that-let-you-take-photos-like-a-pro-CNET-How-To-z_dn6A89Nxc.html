even white balance camera plus also lets you shoot photos in raw one of the best features of camera plus is the ability to tap with two fingers to set exposure and focus separately and as far as there are also tons of other apps with similar feature sets such as moment manual Muse cam halide and even vsco cam another manual camera app that's a bit of a step up in terms of features and difficulty is pro cam 5 you get the same controller for white balance manual focus ISO and shutter speed but you can also choose between Raw and TIFF and there are several modes to choose between 360 exposure histogram and focus peaking at first glance pro cam can feel like information overload but for photographers who are used to shooting in manual and having total control all of these settings HDR low light zoom and high res one of the best features of Hydra is the high-res mode which borrows a trick from Astro photography by snapping multiple photos rapidly and using these slight differences in each to fill in the blanks which turns a 12 megapixel photo into a 32 megapixel image and it works very well finally slow shutter specializes in long exposures you can choose between
