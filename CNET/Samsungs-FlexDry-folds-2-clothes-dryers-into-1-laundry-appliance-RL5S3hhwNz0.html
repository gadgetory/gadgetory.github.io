Samsung's FlexDry folds 2 clothes dryers into 1 laundry appliance

2017-05-30
I don't think anyone really likes to do laundry and one of the biggest pains is having to dry different items at different times the new Samsung flex dry is built to make life a little easier at least in that regard this machine is essentially two dryers in one a main drum sits down below and handles regular dryer duties up top those where things get interesting here you'll find a smaller compartment designed to dry delicate items which can't be tumbled the Flex dry has Wi-Fi connectivity too so you can monitor and control both units from your phone and as you'd expect there are plenty of dryer cycles to choose from plus steam and D wrinkle modes it's a solid performer as well not the speediest dryer we've tested but it's still a lot faster than many you can't deny that for a dryer the Samsung flex dry is unique and innovative unfortunately priced at $1,900 it's also very expensive between its two compartments it has a total capacity of 7.5 cubic feet that's less than the eight or even nine cubic feet some other large dryers offer you also can't stack it on top or below another unit to save space still if you have lots of money to spend in a big laundry room this is a very attractive and capable option
