Nokia 6 is pure Android Nougat in a sleek aluminium body

2017-02-26
if you had a cellphone around the turn of the millennium there's a good chance it was a Nokia and now after disappearing what look to be forever almost three years ago our trusty friend is back at least in a way the Nokia six isn't a sturdy stubby candy bar phone with a wacky keyboard or a battery that can last forever it has a polished skin that's curved from a single block of aluminium and runs of pure version of Android nougat that means no bloatware or special skins both of which are welcome moves simplicity rules here which is a classic Nokia touch it will come in two shades of black plus silver blue and copper the 5.5 inch displays Full HD and covered by Gorilla Glass most of the features inside we've seen before you'll find a 16-megapixel camera a fingerprint sensor dual speakers and an amplifier with Dolby Atmos tech there's also a Nokia six art a black limited edition which has more memory and storage and a slightly glossy black finish but otherwise the phones are the same Nokia 6 is already sold in China but both phones will be landing in more countries soon
