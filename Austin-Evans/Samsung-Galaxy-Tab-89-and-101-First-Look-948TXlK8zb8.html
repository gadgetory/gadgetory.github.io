Samsung Galaxy Tab 8.9 and 10.1 First Look

2011-03-22
today at CTIA Samsung unveiled the two latest tablets the Samsung Galaxy Tab 8.9 and 10.1 to start with let's take a look at the Samsung Galaxy Tab 8.9 which features a one gigahertz dual-core processor in Android 3.0 honeycomb the operating system is slightly customized with Samsung's TouchWiz interface which changes some of the styling along with the new mini apps tray in additional widgets as the name implies it features an 8.9 inch display with a resolution of 1280 by 800 which is quite impressive for the size you'll also find a 3 megapixel rear facing camera with autofocus and flash as well as AI front-facing 2-megapixel camera the tablet will ship in 16 and 32 gigabyte capacities for 469 and 569 dollars respectively making them very competitive especially when you consider that they both have microSD card slots for expansion next we have the Samsung Galaxy Tab 10.1 which has been slightly redesigned from the version announced last month it features the same processor from the 8.9 as well as honeycomb in the same pair of cameras where it differs is with larger 10.1 inch screen which also has a resolution of 1280 by 800 you'll pay a slight premium at 499 and 599 for 16 and 32 gigabyte models however that's still quite reasonable both tablets are also eight point six millimeters thin making the Galaxy Tab line even thinner than the iPad 2 so that's it personally I'm very impressed with what Samsung announced as both models of the Galaxy Tab are competitively priced and offered very impressive features and thinness while pricing for the 3G models wasn't announced you can expect to see both of these tablets available this summer
