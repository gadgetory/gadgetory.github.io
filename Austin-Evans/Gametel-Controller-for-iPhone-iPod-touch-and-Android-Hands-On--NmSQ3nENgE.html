Gametel Controller for iPhone, iPod touch and Android Hands On

2012-01-08
hey guys this is Austin and today I'm here with a hands-on of the game tail controller for iPhone iPod Touch iPad and Android devices the game tell is very similar to the eye control pad with a few physical buttons on the device and an adjustable clip for using with a wide variety of supported smartphones just pair the controller with your device and open a supported app and you'll be able to control it with the game tell up front you'll find a decent d-pad for buttons as well as a pair of shoulder buttons in my short hands on time quality seem to be pretty decent if not spectacular and there was no detectable lag the big issue with the game tell is supported games as each game will need to support the Bluetooth controls whoever the iCade suite of games will work as well as a few others such as no gravity and silverfish the game tel should be available in the United States soon for about $50
