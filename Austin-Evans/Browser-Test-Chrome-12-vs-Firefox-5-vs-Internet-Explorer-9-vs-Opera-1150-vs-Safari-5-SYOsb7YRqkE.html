60 frames per second and Firefox does a respectable job while the others fall flat unlike previous tests there is no overwhelming winner at this time each browser with the exception of Safari had at least one area where it did a great job however if I had to pick just one it would be chrome opera impressed me thoroughly with its performance and excellent interface Internet Explorer remains one of
