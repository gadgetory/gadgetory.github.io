Killzone 3: First Hour Impressions

2011-02-22
hey guys this is Kevin with tech post lunch and dinner with our first our impressions of Killzone 3 for the ps3 first off it must be said the game's visuals are absolutely astounding give them the game a great gritty cinematic feel the single-player campaign so far while being fairly standard for an FPS setting sets up the storyline interesting in a fashion nelsa does a great job of introducing new players to the game in terms of not only controls but also helping catch you up to speed on the main story as well the game relies on a first-person cover mode of play that can be a little awkward at first but allows for players to use cover effectively while still having the accuracy afforded by first-person view point online modes feature the standard team deathmatch as well as a mission mode and war zone mode which has teams compete over a series of varying objectives such as projecting or assassinating particular player as well as controlling zones or just trying to get a larger kill count in a set amount of time players are able to use five different classes online which can grow with more matches allowing them to unlock new weapons and abilities for each class now this is only what I've experienced after one hour of gameplay and once I've sent a lot more time into it I'll let you guys know more about the campaign mode and any other features I discover online as well as how fun the game ends up being overall till then I'm coming for tldtoday thanks you you
