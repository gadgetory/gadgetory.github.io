Dead Space 2: First Impressions

2011-01-26
hey guys this is kevin from tech miss lunch and dinner with our first impressions of dead space two for the xbox 360 ps3 and pc now i'll have to admit I didn't actually play the first dead space however two helps make up for this fact by offering a short movie that summarizes the events from one and sets up for the beginning of two when you start playing the game you get thrown into the action almost immediately dealing with a combination of running for your life hallucinations dark rooms with eerie noises and eventually getting a decent enough set of equipment fight back against the Necromorphs you encounter the graphics thus far well done and the first our featured global design that made great use of both lighting and sounds to help give a great sci-fi horror feeling of never knowing what's going to be around the next corner combat in the game is fairly standard for an over-the-shoulder shooter and offers a number of special abilities to help deal with enemies such as telekinesis and the ability to slow down objects or enemies overall gameplay has been solid and my only complaint really is that telekinesis can get a little awkward when you aim for one item somehow grab another I'll let you guys know if the game keeps the pace up and how much fun it is overall in our full review once I'm finished so then I'm coming for tea love you today thanks for watching
