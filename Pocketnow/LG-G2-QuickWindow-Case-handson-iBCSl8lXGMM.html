LG G2 QuickWindow Case hands-on

2013-08-07
a high-end flagship smartphone deserves a special carrying solution but this time it's not called a flip cover but a quick window case this is michael fisher along with adam lane from pocket now let's take a gander at this LG made accessory for the LG g2 let's get this out of the way right up front yes the quick window case bears a striking resemblance to Samsung's flip cover for the Galaxy s4 but there are some differences here aside from the material and the window size worth noting LG has taken the software customization much more seriously than Samsung here with a bevy of customizable clocks and Status screens that can be changed with a simple swipe yes the screen only illuminates behind the window to save battery life and there's also the capability of locking and unlocking the device with the dual tap we showed you in our hands-on video the case also comes in a variety of colors and it will launch alongside the g2 when it lands later this year so maybe not too much in the way of differences but overall a powerful accessory for what looks to be a powerful flagship once again Michael Fisher and Adam Lane from PocketNow thanks for watching stay tuned for more
