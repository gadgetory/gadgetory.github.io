a smaller package the screen is larger than last years and stocked with improvements five inches on the diagonal Super AMOLED and 1080p with the pixel density of 441 pixels per inch in person it's stunningly beautiful sandwiched between it and the removable 2600 milliamp hour battery on our American review unit is a Qualcomm Snapdragon 600 processor clocked at 1.9 gigahertz backed up by the
