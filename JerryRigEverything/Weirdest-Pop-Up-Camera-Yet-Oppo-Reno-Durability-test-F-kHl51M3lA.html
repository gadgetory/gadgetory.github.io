Let's scratch test the screen. On my standardized durability test, I have a set of Mohs picks that tell what hardness level the screen in. Level 3 is plastic. Level 6 is glass, and level 8 is sapphire. To my surprise, the Oppo Reno started scratching at a level 3. They very effectively hid a high quality screen protector on the front. No complaints here...I like protection. The Reno series also has this weird niblet looking bump thing on the back. It's called an O-Dot and is supposedly made from ceramic. The same test which obliterated the last Oppo phone that came across my desk. The Oppo Find X died.
