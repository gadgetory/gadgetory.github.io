we find an SD card slot and microphone input for when you want to attach an external mic the micro camera is capable of raw recording as well as many flavors of Apple ProRes for power Blackmagic has gone with the widely used Canon LP e6 battery which many shooters will already have plenty up from using Canon DSLRs Blackmagic also announced a second variation on the micro camera which has an ever so slightly larger sensor that is 4k capable this version of the camera is geared more towards broadcast production trading its internal SD card recording slot for a 6g SDI in and out while not a direct competitor to say an action camera this camera is a good alternative if you need a high quality image that records to a more robust codec than others in the market I can see this camera being used as a crash cam or a high-quality way to get shots that you wouldn't be able to fit a traditional camera in at a price point of 995 dollars for the standard version and 1295 for the 4k version
